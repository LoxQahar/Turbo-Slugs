Introduction

At Lox Turbo Slugs, your privacy matters. This page outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (EU 2016/679), also known as GDPR.

What Data We Collect

  • Your name, email address, and phone number (via contact or order forms)
  • Shipping and billing address (for purchases)
  • Technical data like your IP address, browser, and device information (via analytics)
  • Preferences and purchase history (to improve your experience)

How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Process your orders and ship products
  • Send transactional or promotional emails (only with your consent)
  • Respond to customer support inquiries
  • Improve website functionality and user experience through analytics

Cookies

Our website uses cookies to remember your preferences and track traffic via tools like Google Analytics. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

How We Protect Your Data

We store your personal information securely and only allow access to authorized personnel. Industry-standard practices such as SSL encryption and secure servers are used to protect your data.

Your Rights Under GDPR

As an EU citizen, you have the right to:

  • Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Withdraw your consent at any time
  • Object to data processing for marketing or analytics
